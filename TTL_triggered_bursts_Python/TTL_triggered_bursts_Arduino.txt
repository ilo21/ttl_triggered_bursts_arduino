HARDWARE SETUP:
TTL signal from (BNC cable) should be connected to PIN A0 and GND 
For camera (BNC cable) should be connected to PIN A2 and GND 

SCRIPT:
Start the script and define Frequency, High pulse duration and how long should the series of sent pulses last. 
click on "Start" button (or enter) to start waiting for the TTL signal (on A0)
click on "Stop" button (or enter) to stop waiting for the TTL signal (on A0)
